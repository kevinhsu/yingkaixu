NaHuiKou (NHK)

  • Vice President
    • Oct 2014 - Jun 2015.
  • NHK is a mobile app developed by Glenmere Technology Ltd. The app takes the commission (termed “cashback”) usually paid by retailers to third-party referrers and passes it on to NHK members. NHK provides members exclusive access to rewards from all their favourite brands wherever they shop. Glenmere Technology Ltd is a London-based startup founded in 2014. NHK app is available to download from Apple Store and Google Play.

Campus Seller

  • Co-founder
    • Feb 2014 - May 2014.
  • Campus Seller is a new and local service within campuses which allows students and staff to dispose any goods that they no longer want and wish to sell. The aim is to maximise the value of second hand goods and build a sustainable and recycling model. The project obtained £2,000 grants in total from Student Venture Challenge (SVC) and UnLtd to launch the business and won a finalist place in National Apprentice Challenge (NAC) which is the largest live competition for entrepreneurial students across UK. Knowledge Exchange and Innovation Awards also nominated this project in 2014.
